What is the structure of your dataset?¶

There are 453159 entries for bike bookings in 2017. They have the numerical variable of duration in sec, the timestamp data for the start and end time of the session. Futhermore there are some categorical variables like the gender of the user and the user type. The user type is divided in subscriber and customer.

What is/are the main feature(s) of interest in your dataset?¶

In which month are the most bikes taken? On which days are the most bikes booked, to which time and how does this change over the month?

Does the rental dime differ on weekend compare to the weekdays?

Are males and females taking different rental times?

How do customers compare to subscribers?

What features in the dataset do you think will help support your investigation into your feature(s) of interest?¶

I think the duration time will be the most important feature. Also i am assuming that the daytime of renting will be a good indicator for usage of the bikes.
